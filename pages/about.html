About

svift is a data visualization tool that helps you turn numbers into beautiful charts. svift helps you produce engaging data-driven content without being a data or design expert.

If you need to have a chart ready for your next article or twitter post within the next few minutes, svift is your choice: Make an interactive chart for an online article, produce data-driven content for your social media posts or create a beautiful infographic for your print product.

svift is designed and developed by Sebastian Meier, Alsino Skowronnek and Hans Hack in Berlin.

We are grateful for initial financial support provided by the MIZ Babelsberg to support the development of our first prototype.
